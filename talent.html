Mtaa Art Gallery

These arts were drawn by Kevin Juma. A 29 year old male, who is personate about art. He has been drawing since he left high school. He wishes to start his own art gallery studio to encourage young youths, who are love art not to give up on their talents. In his studio, He has managed to recruit a few students. Mtaa Art Gallery is attracting more and more individuals as the days goes by. Now they have a place where they can express their themselves, the numbers are growing and there is need for more space and more art materials.

We Can dance

They were homeless, they are orphans but that did not stop them from diving into their talents. These kids dance their way to fame. Now they are the heroes to others kids who are in the same situation as they were.

Academics

In 2020 alone; We managed to sponsor 3000 students. Some in campus, secondary schools and others in primary level. Among those in the universities 350 students graduated while 270 are still studying. In general more than three quater of the students were able to join the intended institutions. Our students are among the best performing in the county.
